The dachshund also known as the wiener dog, badger dog, or sausage dog, is a short-legged, long-bodied, hound-type dog breed.
The standard sized dachshund was developed to scent, chase, and flush out badgers and other burrow-dwelling animals, while the miniature dachshund was bred to hunt small animals such as rabbits and mice.


In the Western United States, they have also been used to track wounded deer and hunt prairie dogs.
